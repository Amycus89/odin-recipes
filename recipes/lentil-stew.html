Lentil Stew

Healthy, filling, tasty, and most importantly, cheap.

Creamy and hearty lentil stew warms a cold autumn evening nicely. The stew is flavored with garlic, curry and sambal oelek. Serve with rice, halved cherry tomatoes and top with fresh coriander.

Ingridients:

  • Rice (300 g)
  • Onion (1 large)
  • Olive oil (1 tbsp)
  • Garlic (1 clove)
  • Curry (1 tsp)
  • Tomato paste (2 tbsp)
  • Water (2.5 dl)
  • Lentils, dried (2 dl)
  • Sambal oelek (1 tsp)
  • Canned tomatoes (500 g)
  • Salt (1 pinch)
  • Pepper (1 pinch)
  • Coriander (1 dl)

Steps:

  1. Boil the rica according to the instructions on the package.
  2. Peel and chop the onion. Fry it in the oil in a pot. Press the garlic and add the curry and tomato puree and fry for another minute.
  3. Stir and add the water, lentils and sambal oelek. Let it boil for 15 min until the lentils become soft. Add more water if needed.
  4. Season with salt and pepper according to your preference.
  5. Serve the stew together with the rice, adding some coriander on top.

Enjoy!

Tip: Add some dried ginger as well.
